Typography

Four tiers — Headline, Title, Body, Caption. Sizes stay fixed at every breakpoint.

Headline

Marketing hero, section titles, product pages.

  • Headline large font-headline-large
  • Headline medium font-headline-medium
  • Headline small font-headline-small

Title

Section titles, article headers, cards.

  • Title large font-title-large
  • Title medium font-title-medium
  • Title small font-title-small

Body

Paragraphs and running text. Each tier has an -emphasized variant at weight 600.

  • Body large — the quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. font-body-large
  • Body medium — the quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. font-body-medium
  • Body small — the quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. font-body-small

Caption

Labels, metadata, hints. Also has an -emphasized variant.

  • Caption medium — labels, metadata. font-caption-medium
  • Caption small — timestamps, hints. font-caption-small

Using across breakpoints

Sizes are fixed per tier. For responsive text, swap tokens per breakpoint — e.g. headline-medium on desktop, title-large on mobile. 

.hero-title {
  font-size: var(--font-headline-medium-size);
  line-height: var(--font-headline-medium-lh);
}

@media (max-width: 640px) {
  .hero-title {
    font-size: var(--font-title-large-size);
    line-height: var(--font-title-large-lh);
  }
}
