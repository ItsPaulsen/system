What to look for

Ruter's RGB-channel deltas make hue drift — subtracting a constant from each channel of a tinted color shifts its hue slightly because the ratio between channels changes. Our oklab mix stays truer to the base hue (it moves through perceptual lightness only). If our chips look "cleaner" or "purer" — same hue, just different brightness — that's the win. If they look flatter or muddier compared to Ruter, oklab isn't paying off here.
